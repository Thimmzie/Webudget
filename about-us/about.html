About WeBudget

At WeBudget, we believe that money management should be simple, transparent, and stress-free—whether you're budgeting alone, with a partner, roommates, or family.

What is WeBudget ?

Our platform is designed to automate expense tracking, simplify bill splitting, and help you stay in control of your finances without the hassle of manual entries. With features like SMS transaction tracking, receipt uploads, and optional bank syncing, WeBudget ensures that every expense is accounted for effortlessly.

Our Mission

WeBudget simplifies money management for couples and cohabitants with smart tools for shared budgets, bill splitting, and expense tracking.

Our History

Born from the need for better shared finance management, WeBudget helps users track expenses, split bills, and save together with ease..

Our Vision

To empower individuals and households with seamless, collaborative budgeting tools that foster financial transparency, accountability, and stability.
