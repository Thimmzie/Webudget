Frequently Asked Questions

What is WeBudget?

WeBudget is a budgeting platform designed to help families, couples, and cohabitants manage shared finances, track expenses, budget efficiently, and plan financial goals together.

Who can use WeBudget?

WeBudget is for families, couples, roommates, and any group managing shared expenses or financial goals together.

Is WeBudget free to use?

WeBudget offers a free version with essential budgeting tools. Premium plans provide advanced features like automated insights, multi-user collaboration, and investment tracking.

Can I use WeBudget alone?

Yes, WeBudget can be used for personal budgeting, but its full potential is realized when managing finances with others.

How does shared budgeting work?

You can create a shared financial space, invite members, set budgets, track expenses, and manage financial goals collaboratively.

Can I link my bank accounts to WeBudget?

Yes, WeBudget allows secure bank linking to automatically track expenses and balances.

Does WeBudget help with savings?

Yes, you can set savings goals, allocate funds, and get insights on spending patterns to optimize savings.

What happens if I exceed my budget?

You’ll get a notification when you’re close to or over your budget, so you can adjust your spending.

Does WeBudget store my financial information?

WeBudget does not store sensitive banking credentials. All financial data remains encrypted and private.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You’ll retain access to free features, but premium tools will be locked after your billing cycle ends.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email support, live chat, or phone support through the Help Center.

Can I use WeBudget on different devices

Yes, WeBudget syncs across desktop and mobile devices for seamless access.
